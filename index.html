About Us

GamerX is more than just a regular video game store. It was founded by a group of gaming enthusiasts who have a passion for sharing their love of gaming with others. Gaming has been a part of our lives for years and we've been fascinated by the way it brings people together. Over the years, we have witnessed firsthand how gaming has evolved and how it continues to grow. That is why at GamerX, we offer some of the most sought-after gaming products on the market.


Our origins in the gaming industry stem from the PlayStation®2 and the iconic PSP. We know firsthand the joy of playing classic multiplayer games like Need For Speed: Most Wanted, Dragon Ball Z Budokai Tenkaichi 3, and FIFA with family and friends. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just starting, our wide selection of games, consoles, and accessories are perfect for anyone who wants to elevate their gaming experience and we can promise that you'll love our curated gaming store and as well as a knowledgeable staff who can help you find exactly what you're looking for.
